Mohamed Salah's Records

Club Records with Liverpool

  • Most Goals in a Premier League Season (38 Games): 32 goals (2017–18).
  • Fastest Liverpool Player to 100 Premier League Goals: 151 games.
  • First Liverpool Player to Score in Nine Consecutive Matches (2021).
  • Most Goals in a UEFA Champions League Season for Liverpool: 10 goals (2017–18).
  • First Liverpool Player to Score 20+ Goals in Six Consecutive Seasons.
  • First Liverpool Player to Score in Five Consecutive Opening Premier League Matches (2021).
  • Most Goals by an African Player in Premier League History: Surpassed Didier Drogba in 2021.

Premier League Records

  • Most Goals in a Debut Premier League Season: 32 goals (2017–18).
  • Most Player of the Month Awards in a Single Season: 3 (2017–18).
  • First Player to Score 100+ Left-Footed Goals in the Premier League.
  • Most Goals Scored by an African Player in Premier League History.
  • Most Games Scored in During a Single Premier League Season: 24 games (2017–18).
  • Most Goals Scored on the Opening Day of Premier League Seasons: 8 goals.

International Records

  • Egypt’s All-Time Leading Scorer in FIFA World Cup Qualifiers.
  • Scored in Egypt’s First FIFA World Cup Appearance in 28 Years (2018).

UEFA Champions League Records

  • First Egyptian to Score in a Champions League Final (2018).
  • Fastest Goal Scored in UEFA Champions League Final History: 2 minutes vs. Tottenham (2019).
  • Most Goals Scored by an African Player in Champions League History: Surpassed Didier Drogba’s record.

Global and Miscellaneous Records

  • First African Player to Score 30+ Goals in a Premier League Season.
  • First Egyptian Player to Win the Premier League.
  • Most Goals Scored for an English Club in the Champions League.
  • First Player to Score a Hat-Trick at Old Trafford in the Premier League Era (2021).
  • Highest-Scoring African Player in Europe’s Top Five Leagues.

Personal Achievements and Milestones

  • FIFA Puskás Award: 2018 (for his stunning goal against Everton).
  • Time 100 Most Influential People: 2019.
  • BBC African Footballer of the Year: 2017, 2018.
  • CAF African Player of the Year: 2017, 2018.
  • Golden Foot Award: 2021.
