Mohamed Salah, often hailed as one of the greatest footballers of his generation, has left an indelible mark on the sport. Here is a comprehensive list of Mohamed Salah's notable achievements at both individual and team levels:

Club Achievements

Liverpool (United Kingdom)

  • Premier League: 2019–20
  • UEFA Champions League: 2018–19
  • FIFA Club World Cup: 2019
  • UEFA Super Cup: 2019
  • FA Cup: 2021–22
  • EFL Cup (Carabao Cup): 2021–22
  • Community Shield: 2022

AS Roma (Italy)

Helped secure a second-place finish in Serie A (2016–17), the club's best performance in year

Basel (Switzerland)

Swiss Super League: 2012–13, 2013–14

International Achievements (Egypt)

  • Africa Cup of Nations Runner-up: 2017, 2021
  • Olympic Games Participation: 2012
  • Played a pivotal role in Egypt’s qualification for the 2018 FIFA World Cup, ending a 28-year drought.
  • UEFA Super Cup: 2019
